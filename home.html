Karate

Karate is a Japanese martial art whose physical aspects seek the development of defensive and counterattacking body movements.

Jiu-Jitsu

Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling orientated martial art/combat sport that evolved from its Japanese roots in 1920’s Brazil, and continued to evolve for many decades after until its present format. Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling orientated martial art/combat sport that evolved from its Japanese roots in 1920’s Brazil, and continued to evolve for many decades after until its present format.

Wrestling

The sport of wrestling has been around for thousands of years and Greco-Roman wrestlers competed in the first modern Olympic games held in Athens in 1896.

UFC

The Ultimate Fighting Championship is a mixed-martial arts promotion in which a series of fights take place around the world, also known as fight nights. It calls itself the "premier organisation in MMA".

Boxing

Boxing, sport, both amateur and professional, involving attack and defense with the fists. Boxers usually wear padded gloves and generally observe the code set forth in the marquess of Queensberry rules.
